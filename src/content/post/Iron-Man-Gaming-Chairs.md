### What features are common in Iron Man gaming chairs?

Iron Man gaming chairs often have features like high-quality leather or PU upholstery, adjustable armrests, a retractable footrest, a high backrest for lumbar support, and a sturdy base with smooth-rolling casters. ### How can I clean an Iron Man gaming chair?

Cleaning instructions may vary depending on the material of your gaming chair. For leather or PU-covered chairs, use a soft, damp cloth to wipe away dirt and dust. Avoid using harsh chemicals or solvents, as these can damage the upholstery. For fabric-covered chairs, vacuum the chair using the upholstery attachment to remove dust and debris. Spot clean any stains with a mild, soapy solution and a soft cloth or sponge.
