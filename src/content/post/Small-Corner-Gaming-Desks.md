* Efficient cable management solutions

### Considerations

Before making your purchase, take into account these factors: 

* Your available space and desk dimensions

* Your preferred positioning (whether you want a left or right corner unit)

* The desk's weight capacity, especially if you plan on having multiple monitors or other heavy peripherals

* Whether the desk is easy to assemble and comes with quality customer support


### General Advice

To ensure the best possible gaming experience, consider the following tips: 

* Choose a desk with a sturdy and solid construction to prevent unwanted wobbling

* Ensure there's enough space between your face and the monitor(s) to maintain good posture and eye health

* Consider purchasing cable sleeves or a cable tray to keep your setup organized

* Make sure the desk has a flat, even surface that won't compromise the performance of your mouse and keyboard
