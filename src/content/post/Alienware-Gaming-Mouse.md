* **Polling Rate:**  This refers to how often the mouse communicates with your computer. The higher the polling rate, the smoother your cursor movement will be. Aim for a polling rate of at least 1,000Hz for seamless gaming.

* **Weight:**  The weight of the mouse can impact your grip and comfort while playing. Some models allow you to adjust the weight for optimal control.

* **Customizable Buttons:**  Having extra programmable buttons on your gaming mouse can provide quick access to in-game functions and commands, improving your overall performance.
