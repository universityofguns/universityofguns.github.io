### What is an IWB gun holster?

An IWB (Inside the Waistband) gun holster is a type of concealed carry holster that attaches to the inside of your waistband. It's designed to securely hold your firearm close to your body for easy access and concealment underneath your clothing. 


### How do IWB gun holsters differ from OWB (Outside the Waistband) holsters?

IWB holsters are discreet, offering better concealment as they keep the firearm close to your body. OWB holsters, on the other hand, attach to the outside of your waistband and are typically more accessible since they don't require you to lift your clothing to draw the weapon.

### What material is best for IWB gun holsters?

Leather and Kydex are popular materials for IWB holsters. Both offer durability and functionality, though Kydex is typically more lightweight and rigid, providing quicker access. 


### How should I choose the right size for my IWB gun holster?

1. Find the exact make and model of your firearm.

2. Search for a holster that is specifically designed for your firearm model, as that will provide the best fit and function.

3. Consider getting a custom fit if your firearm has unique attributes such as a light or laser attached.

### How do I securely attach an IWB gun holster?

The majority of IWB holsters use clips or loops to attach to your waistband or belt. To ensure a secure fit, make sure the clip or loop is tight around your waistband or belt. Some holsters also come with adjustable retention screws, allowing you to fine-tune the grip on your firearm. 


### Which side should I wear an IWB gun holster?

You can choose to wear your IWB gun holster on either the strong side (right-hand draw for right-handed individuals, left-hand draw for left-handed individuals) or the support side (opposite side of your dominant hand). The choice depends on your level of comfort, concealment preferences, and dominant hand strength. 


### How can I ensure proper retention of my firearm in the IWB holster?

* Choose a holster specifically designed for your firearm model, as it will offer the best fit and retention.

* Adjust the retention screws, if applicable, to achieve the desired level of grip on your firearm.

* Ensure a proper fit of the holster on your waistband or belt by tightening the clips or loops securely.


### How do I care for and maintain my IWB gun holster?

Caring for your IWB holster is crucial for maintaining its durability and effectiveness. Regularly inspect the holster for signs of wear or damage, and promptly replace damaged parts or holsters. Always store your IWB holster in a cool and dry place, away from direct sunlight or extreme temperatures.
