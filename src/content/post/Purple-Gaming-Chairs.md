### How are gaming chairs different from regular office chairs?

Gaming chairs are typically more ergonomic and supportive than regular office chairs. They often have more adjustable features like reclining, tilt tension control, and adjustable armrests to ensure prolonged comfort during extended gaming sessions. ### What is the difference between a pedestal and a four-legged gaming chair?

A pedestal gaming chair has a single post that connects the seat to the base, while a four-legged gaming chair has four legs that provide a sturdier and more stable foundation. Pedestal chairs offer slightly more seat movement and are often more compact, making them suitable for smaller spaces. Four-legged chairs provide better stability and are often more suitable for gamers who need to lean back frequently.
