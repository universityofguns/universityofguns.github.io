### What is a Bulldog Gun Holster?

A Bulldog Gun Holster is a special type of holster designed to carry small concealed firearms. It is named "Bulldog" after a popular model of. 44 caliber revolver of the same name, produced by American manufacturer Iver Johnson in the early 20th century. The holster provides secure and comfortable carrying of these compact handguns, usually worn inside the waistband (IWB) or as a shoulder holster.

### What is the difference between IWB and OWB (Outside Waistband) Bulldog Gun Holsters?

OWB Bulldog Gun Holsters, on the other hand, are worn outside the waistband, usually on the strong side (right side for right-handed shooters). They may offer quicker access to your weapons but are less suitable for concealed carry.
