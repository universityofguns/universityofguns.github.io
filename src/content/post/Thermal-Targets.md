## FAQ

### What are thermal targets?

Thermal targets are devices that make animals or other objects appear warmer than they are, allowing hunters to see them more easily in low-light conditions using thermal imaging technology. 


### How do thermal targets improve visibility during night hunts?

Thermal targets enhance visibility in night hunts by emitting heat much like living animals or people, making these targets appear warm and stand out against the cooler background. This allows hunters to locate and engage their targets more easily.

### How do I install a thermal target?

The installation process of a thermal target may vary depending on the specific device. In general, you need to find a suitable location that is clear of obstructions and away from any reflective surfaces. Attach the target using the provided mounting hardware, power on the device, and ensure it emits heat effectively. Follow the manufacturer's instructions for specific installation guidelines. 


### Are there different types of thermal targets available?

Yes, there are various types of thermal targets available in the market, including human silhouette, animal (deer, dove, rabbit, etc. ), moving, and static targets. Each type serves a specific purpose and helps hunters improve their skills in different circumstances.
