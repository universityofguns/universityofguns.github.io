### What is DPI and why is it important for gaming?

DPI (Dots Per Inch) refers to the sensitivity of a mouse. A higher DPI means the mouse cursor moves further on the screen with less physical movement of the mouse. For gamers, this can translate into quicker, more precise movements in-game.

Cleaning your Pulsar gaming mouse is easy. Simply unplug it or remove the battery if it's wireless, then use a soft, damp cloth to gently wipe down the surface of the mouse. If necessary, you can use a cotton swab dipped in rubbing alcohol to clean between buttons and other small spaces.
