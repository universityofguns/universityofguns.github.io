### What is the difference between laser and optical sensors?

Laser sensors have a higher potential DPI range but can be more prone to sensor issues, while optical sensors are generally more stable and accurate. ### What is the importance of DPI in a gaming mouse?

DPI (dots per inch) is a crucial factor in determining how sensitive your gaming mouse is. Higher DPI allows for faster cursor movements, while lower DPI provides more control and precision. Depending on the games you play, you may prefer a higher or lower DPI setting. Some gaming mice allow you to adjust the DPI dynamically, enabling you to switch between precise movements and rapid cursor control as needed.
