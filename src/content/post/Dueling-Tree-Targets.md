## FAQ


### What are Dueling Tree Targets?

<div><a href="https://serp.ly/@universityofguns/amazon/dueling-tree-targets?utm_source=universityofguns&utm_medium=organic&utm_campaign=gh-pages"><img src="https://imagedelivery.net/vy2bglCGN6hEeWOnSe2c7A/Dueling+Tree+Targets-4/w=720,h=540,fit=pad,background=black" alt="Dueling Tree Targets-4"></a></div>

Dueling Tree Targets are a type of reactive target designed for shooting practice and competition. They consist of two or more spinning targets mounted on a tree-like structure. As bullets hit the targets, they spin, allowing for repeat shots without the need for manual resetting. 


### How do Dueling Tree Targets improve shooting skills?

Dueling Tree Targets help improve shooting accuracy and speed by providing a challenging and engaging practice experience. The spinning targets require quick reflexes and precision, making them an ideal tool for developing hand-eye coordination and honing shooting skills.
