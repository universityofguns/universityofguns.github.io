### What is an automatic gun holster?

An automatic gun holster, also known as a quick-draw holster or a self-drawing holster, is a type of gun holster that automatically presents the firearm when the wearer draws it. This design facilitates faster and smoother access to the weapon, making it popular among individuals who prioritize speed and efficiency in handling their firearms. 


### How do automatic gun holsters work?

Automatic gun holsters utilize internal mechanisms, typically spring-loaded or lever-operated systems, to release and present the firearm when the wearer grabs the grip. Once the weapon is drawn, these systems automatically retract and secure the holster's retention mechanism, allowing for a swift yet secure means of accessing the firearm.
