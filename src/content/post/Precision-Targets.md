### What is the difference between steel and paper targets?

Steel targets are made from robust and corrosion-resistant steel, often used for long-lasting performance across various seasons and weather conditions. They usually offer instant audible feedback upon being hit, making them ideal for instantaneous learning and quick identification of shooter errors. Conversely, paper targets are versatile and low-cost, with printable designs allowing customization for specific shooting needs or practice scenarios. Paper targets provide excellent visibility for precise aiming and generally serve as a cost-effective solution for many shooters and enthusiasts. 


### How do I maintain and store Precision Targets properly?

To prolong the lifespan of your Precision Targets, it is crucial to follow the manufacturer's maintenance recommendations. This may include periodic cleaning and inspections for damages. Proper storage requires keeping targets in a cool, dry, and protected area, away from direct sunlight or extreme temperature fluctuations. Additionally, it's essential to store steel targets unloaded and separate them from ammunition to ensure safety and prevent accidental discharge.
