### What is DPI and how does it affect my gaming performance?

DPI (dots per inch) is a measure of how sensitive a mouse is to movement. Higher DPI means the mouse cursor will move faster and more precisely on your screen with less physical movement. This is important for gamers, as it can improve the speed and accuracy of their in-game movements. However, the optimal DPI varies depending on the individual user and the type of game being played. 


### Can I customize the buttons on a USB gaming mouse?

Yes, many USB gaming mice allow you to customize the functions of their buttons to suit your specific needs and preferences. This can be done using software provided by the manufacturer, which may include additional features like DPI adjustment, polling rate customization, and LED lighting control. 


### What is polling rate, and why is it important for gaming?

Polling rate refers to how often a mouse communicates its position to your computer per second. A higher polling rate means more frequent updates, resulting in smoother cursor movement and quicker response times in-game. For gamers, a higher polling rate can provide a competitive advantage by reducing input lag and improving overall responsiveness. 


### How do I clean and maintain a USB gaming mouse?

To clean a USB gaming mouse, first, unplug it from your computer. Use a soft, damp cloth to gently wipe the mouse surface, being careful not to apply too much pressure. You can also use a soft-bristled toothbrush to clean any debris from crevices and small openings. Avoid using any liquid cleaners, as they may damage the mouse components. For more thorough cleaning, refer to the manufacturer's instructions or seek professional assistance.
