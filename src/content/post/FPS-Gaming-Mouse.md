### What is an FPS gaming mouse?

An FPS gaming mouse is a type of computer mouse specifically designed for first-person shooter (FPS) games. These mice offer features such as high DPI (dots per inch) for precise cursor movements, customizable buttons for quick access to important in-game commands, and adjustable weight systems to provide optimal comfort and control during gameplay. 


### What is the difference between a regular mouse and an FPS gaming mouse?

A regular mouse is generally suitable for everyday tasks like browsing the web, office work, and some casual gaming. They often have a basic design with limited customization options. In contrast, an FPS gaming mouse is specifically tailored for first-person shooter games, offering high DPI, customizable buttons, and advanced ergonomic features that enhance gaming performance and provide a more comfortable gaming experience.

### What is DPI, and why is it important for an FPS gaming mouse?

DPI (dots per inch) refers to the sensitivity of a mouse, or how far it moves the cursor on the screen per inch of movement. In FPS games, a high DPI allows for precise and quick movements, giving players an edge in securing accurate shots and reacting faster to opponents. Many gaming mice offer adjustable DPI settings, allowing you to fine-tune the sensitivity to your specific needs.
