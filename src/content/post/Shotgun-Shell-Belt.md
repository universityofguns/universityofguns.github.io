A Shotgun Shell Belt is a specialized accessory designed to carry and organize shotgun shells. Made from durable materials such as nylon or leather, it secures around the waist like a belt, providing easy access to the ammunition during shooting sessions. 


### 2. How does a Shotgun Shell Belt work?

Shotgun Shell Belts feature a series of pouches or holders, each holding usually one to three shells. These holders are evenly distributed around the belt, allowing quick access to the ammunition when needed.
