### What type of gun safes does Sentinel offer?

Sentinel offers a variety of gun safes to cater to different needs and preferences. These include biometric gun safes, key lock gun safes, and electronic lock gun safes. 


### How do biometric gun safes work?

Biometric gun safes use fingerprint recognition technology to grant access. Users can store their fingerprints in the system, and the safe will open only when the correct fingerprint is detected. This eliminates the need for keys or combination locks, making it a convenient and secure option.

### What are the benefits of choosing a key lock gun safe?

Key lock gun safes are reliable and easy to use. They provide a quick access method for gun owners who may need to retrieve their firearms in an emergency situation. Additionally, key lock gun safes are generally more affordable than other locking mechanisms. 


### How do electronic lock gun safes work?

Electronic lock gun safes use a keypad or touchscreen to input a pre-programmed access code. This allows for quick and easy access to the stored firearms while maintaining a secure environment.
