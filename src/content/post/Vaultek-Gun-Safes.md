### Considerations When Buying a Gun Safe

* Height and Width: Measure your desired safe location to ensure compatibility with your space and make sure the safe can be easily accessed.

* Weight: Consider the safe's weight when choosing a suitable location. A heavier safe is more difficult for thieves to steal but may be more challenging to move once installed.

* Ease of Use: Opt for a safe with an intuitive and user-friendly locking mechanism, such as biometric scanners, combination locks, or key-operated locks.


### General Advice for Using Gun Safes

1. Store firearms unloaded and separate from ammunition to reduce the risk of accidental discharge.

2. Regularly inspect your safe for any signs of damage or wear, and perform routine maintenance as necessary.

3. Keep important documents, such as firearm registration paperwork or insurance certificates, in a separate, secure location.
